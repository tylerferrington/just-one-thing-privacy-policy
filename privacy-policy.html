Privacy Policy for Just One Thing

Effective date: 9th August 2025

1. Information We Collect

We designed Just One Thing to be simple and private. We do not collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, or contact details through the app.

The app may store the following data locally on your device:

  • Tasks you create and complete
  • Your task streaks and progress
  • App preferences (e.g., settings)

We may use anonymous analytics (via Firebase or similar tools) to understand how the app is used and improve features. This data:

  • Does not identify you personally
  • May include app usage patterns, device type, and general location (city/region)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use anonymous analytics to:

  • Improve app functionality
  • Identify and fix bugs
  • Understand feature popularity

We do not:

  • Sell your data
  • Share your personal details with third parties
  • Use your data for targeted advertising

3. Data Storage and Security

Your personal tasks and progress are stored locally on your device. Analytics data is processed securely through our analytics provider and handled according to their privacy policies.

4. Third-Party Services
